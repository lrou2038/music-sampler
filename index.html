IT'S TIME TO STREAM JESSIE REYEZ

Photo shot by Mac Downey on Flickr.

Turn your volume up and get ready to stream Jessie Reyez. Reyez is a 28-year-old Columbian-Canadian singer. She captures listeners attention with her raspy voice, heartbreakingly-real lyrics and catchy beats. Her ascent to fame started in 2016 when her single "Figures" went viral on social media. Since then, she's released albums "Being Human in Public" and more recently "Before Love Came to Kill Us". Click on the album covers below to hear a taste of her music.
